Career

I started working as soon as I left school! I had many different interests in my life at the time so I needed to fund my hobbies so that I could ease my parents expenditure! As soon as I turned 16 I took a job where I was waitering at many different venues around the country, i didn't really enjoy this job as I was earning money but was left with no time to actually enjoy my life! I then decided to work hard for a couple months to raise some money and then quit! After that I took up a part time job at primark whilst I was at university to give myself Disposable income and money to travel after studying and now I am early into my career beginning as a software tester with Sparta Global.
